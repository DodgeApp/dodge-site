This Privacy Policy explains how Dodge Labs (Pty) Ltd ("Dodge," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the Dodge mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Dodge, we may collect:

  • Account information: email, username, and profile details you provide.
  • Profile content: profile photo and related metadata.
  • Location information: precise or approximate location data (when permission is enabled).
  • Safety activity data: reports, circle interactions, and related in-app events.
  • Technical data: device/app diagnostics, logs, and performance data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate core app features (account access, profile, circles, and safety functions).
  • Display relevant location-based and circle-based information.
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance of Dodge.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and misuse of the Service.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important service updates.

3. Location Data and Safety Features

If you enable location permissions, Dodge may process your location for core safety and circle functionality. You can change permissions in your device settings at any time, but disabling location may limit features.

Dodge is a community safety tool and not emergency response infrastructure. In emergencies, contact local emergency services.

4. Legal Basis and Consent

We process information based on your consent, to provide the Service you request, to comply with legal obligations, and for our legitimate interests (such as app security and abuse prevention), where permitted by law.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: infrastructure, cloud, authentication, storage, and analytics providers.
  • Other users: profile and safety information visible through app features you use.
  • Legal/compliance parties: when required by law, legal process, or to protect safety and rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods vary by data type and legal requirements.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own depending on where our providers operate. We use appropriate safeguards where required by applicable law.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request data portability where legally applicable.

You can also manage app permissions (location, notifications, etc.) in your device settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Dodge is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information unlawfully, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
